Empowering therapists to extend care beyond the center, into homes

Therapist-driven, personalized sessions for nurturing growth and development at home

CognitiveBotics empowers therapists to support children with neurodiverse conditions like Autism, ADHD, Down Syndrome, or for those who learn differently, helping them learn and grow confidently at their own pace.

Customized learning plan

Therapists design personalized learning plans that cater to each child’s unique needs, guiding their growth based on individual strengths and challenges.

Interactive learning, at home

Therapists lead home-based digital sessions to develop essential skills, while parents receive easy-to-follow training videos for smooth support progress.

Track progress, celebrate growth

Detailed reports keep therapists and parents informed, refining the learning plan to fit the child’s evolving needs.

Skills Empowering Every Child’s Journey

Attending Skill

Enhances a child's ability to focus and maintain attention on tasks or instructions

Imitation Skill

Helps children learn by observing and mimicking others' actions or behaviors

Receptive Skill

Strengthens the child's ability to understand and process spoken language and instructions

Expressive Skill

Develops the child's capability to communicate thoughts, needs, and emotions effectively

Visual Skill

Improves how children interpret and understand visual information from their environment

Social Skill

Builds the ability to interact, communicate, and engage positively with others

Play Skill

Encourages imaginative play and the development of problem-solving and creative thinking

Self-Help Skill

Promotes independence by teaching children essential daily living activities

Reading & Writing

Fosters literacy by improving the ability to read, comprehend, and write

Arithmetic Skill

Enhances basic mathematical understanding and problem-solving abilities

Group Skill

Cultivates cooperation, teamwork, and the ability to work effectively in group settings
